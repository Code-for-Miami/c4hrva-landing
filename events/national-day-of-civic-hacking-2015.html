National Day of Civic Hacking 2015

Saturday, June 6th | 9:30 a.m. to 6:30 p.m.

Thousands of people from across the United States will come together for National Day of Civic Hacking. The event will bring together urbanists, civic hackers, government staff, developers, designers, community organizers and anyone with the passion to make our city better.

Anyone can participate; you don‚Äôt have to be an expert in technology, you just have to care about your neighborhood and community.

We will collaboratively build new solutions using publicly-released data, technology, and design processes to improve our communities and the governments that serve them.

We ask that participants at Code for Miami events follow our Code of Conduct to ensure that our group is a safe and welcoming space.

Questions? Contact our event organizer, Danielle.


Location: The Lab Miami, 400 NW 26th St.

Time: 9:30 a.m. to 6:30 p.m.
